Data visualization

Data vizualization is the way we provide anaylized data to the audience. It is crucial to understand whom you prepare the data to and the vizzes have to be prepared in a such way that it will be clear for your clients or stackholders for example. An ideal way to present your data is to prepare it as story telling.

Many aspects you should keep in mind such as colors, font size or types of plots such waffle plot or histogram, pie chart or bar chart. It is recommended to have 2-4 colors on you dashboard and one should keep in mimd that 1 out of 10 people is color blinded. It applies that we should choose colors in such way that a color blind person will be able to distinguish colors on your plots.
